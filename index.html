Tweaked Conway's Game of Life

Conway's game of life with the addition of "genes". Each cell has a color, which is inherited according to Mendelian genetics from two "parents" upon birth.



This menu lets you set the survival and birth rules of the green cells (genotype 1 1). Watch and see how different rules change the nature of the game!
